Communication

You will be included in communication channels that are relevant to your area of expertise. Please follow the Community Guidelines and Anti-Harassment Policy in your correspondence and interactions with other program members and Googlers.

Global GDG support

Google mailing groups

Please ensure you have been added to the gdg-leads-announce@googlegroups.com mailing lists below within the 7 days of accepting Google Terms and Conditions. To effortlessly locate Google Groups you're a part of, simply click on the link provided.

Being part of these lists allows you to:

  • Receive monthly GDG newsletters and program updates.
  • Access GDP Premium.

On-Demand Support

Email us anytime at gdg-support@google.com we collaborate closely with your local POCs.

GDG regional support

Your chapter's GDG team includes a local Regional Lead and Community Manager. They are your primary contacts for support. To facilitate local collaboration, your Regional Lead will add you to a dedicated group of organizers in your area. This group uses a real-time communication channel like WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, or Slack, based on regional preference, to provide support.
