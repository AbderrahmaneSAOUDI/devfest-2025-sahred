Unlock your cloud potential with the no-cost Get Certified program! In a high-demand market, Google Cloud customers can build and validate skills with a commitment of up to ~9 hours per week. Registration opens August 26, 2025! Complete the learning program and earn a free certification exam voucher (up to $200 value). Ensure you can commit before applying. Following review, you'll be waitlisted for an upcoming session. This exclusive program requires a corporate email with access to Google Workspace apps (Meet, Drive, Groups).

Registration

The Get Certified program requires a Google Developer Profile. If you don't already have a Google Developer Profile, we'll ask you to create one during registration with a Google Account before collecting your current work email and Google Cloud certification preference.

Applications are considered on a first come first served basis so apply as soon as possible. Access to Google Workspace features, including Google Drive, Google Groups, and Google Meet, is required to successfully participate in the program.

Checklist

  1. Create a Google Developer Profile* and select one of the 5 certification paths:
    • Associate Cloud Engineer, available in English, Spanish (Latin America), Portuguese (Brazil), and Bahasa (Indonesia)
    • Professional Cloud Architect, available in English
    • Professional Cloud Architect Express for AWS professionals (4 week program), available in English
    • Professional Cloud Architect Express for Azure professionals (4 week program), available in English
    • Professional Data Engineer, available in English
  2. Expect a status update one week prior to the Edition's start date.
  3. Upon acceptance, look out for an email confirming your cohort start date.
  4. Take advantage of your initial 7-day access to instructor-led training labs and information on progressing to Stage 2.
  5. Engage in the structured 10-week cohort-based learning program for focused progress. and leverage anytime access to Skills Boost for flexible self-study.
  6. Participate in the six live exam sessions for expert guidance.
  7. Achieve your certification!

*A Google Developer Profile grants access to exclusive resources that accelerate your journey to learn, build, and grow at no cost. We recommend logging in and creating it using a personal Google Account because you can take this profile with you throughout your career. At a later step in your application, we’ll ask for your current work email to help prioritize applications for Get Certified.

FAQs

If you do not find the answer to your question, reach out to your Regional Lead or to gdg-support@google.com.

How do I update my application if my company email or desired certification track changes?

keyboard_arrow_up

You’ll need to re-submit your registration, but don’t worry: that’s easy to do. Start by following the instructions here to remove your existing registration. Afterward proceed to https://cloud.google.com/innovators/getcertified and select “Apply Now” to register again. Complete all 3 steps with your new selections. Heads up: if you are using the same work email as your previous registration, you will see a “You applied to Get Certified!” message in the final registration step, but you will not receive a second confirmation email. This is normal, and we have received your updated details.

Who is eligible to participate in the Get Certified program?

keyboard_arrow_up

The Get Certified program has a thorough application review process. All applicants begin on a waitlist and are considered for admission by the Get Certified team. To start the process and receive the most relevant updates and information about the program, please provide your company name and email address.

When is the next cohort for the instructor-led certification training starting?

keyboard_arrow_up

The next cohort starts Oct 13, 2025.

Why do I need to share two email addresses when joining the waitlist?

keyboard_arrow_up

When you register to join the waitlist for Get Certified, you’ll be asked to provide both a personal and organization email address. This is because during registration you will also be creating a Google Developer Profile and joining the Innovators cloud technologist community, if you haven’t already. This profile and community are designed to take with you as you progress through your career, even as you change organizations. Your current organization (e.g. work or school) email may help prioritize you on the waitlist, depending on your organization's relationship with Google Cloud, and will be where you receive communications specifically for the Get Certified program.

How do I drop off the Get Certified waitlist?

keyboard_arrow_up

To remove yourself from the waitlist, please remove the Innovators Get Certified badge from your Developer Profile.

When will I hear back about my registration for the program?

keyboard_arrow_up

The Get Certified program has limited seats. We review applications on a first-come, first-served basis and will update you on your status as soon as possible. Expect a response no later than 7 business days prior to the start of an Edition.

What can I expect if I'm accepted?

keyboard_arrow_up

If accepted, you will receive a confirmation email from googlecloud@google.com. Confirmations are sent on a rolling basis. About 7 days before the program kick-off, you will also receive an invitation at the business email you used to register from one of our training partners to save the date for the instructor-led class. Please note that your class assignment cannot be changed, and the session will not be recorded. Attendance is strongly recommended.

If you cannot find either of these emails in your inbox, please check your “Spam” or “Junk” folder. Otherwise, please reach out to your Google account team contact for assistance.

Can I join multiple certification cohorts in 2025?

keyboard_arrow_up

Yes, however, the Get Certified program has limited seats, so in each Edition we prioritize new learners over those who have already participated in 2025.

What is the Google Cloud Innovators program?

keyboard_arrow_up

The Innovators program is the program for all developers, technical practitioners, students, and users across Google Cloud - including Workspace - to support your personal and professional growth.

How do I join the Innovators program?

keyboard_arrow_up

You can join the Innovators program by first clicking Join the waitlist or Apply here, registering for a Developer Profile using your personal email address, then filling out the required fields to become an Innovator and click the “Become Member” button. From there you will be prompted to finish your registration for the Get Certified waitlist and enter your organization email, email registered In the GDG program, and choosing your certification journey.

How do I control what e-mails I receive?

keyboard_arrow_up

You control the e-mails you receive from the Innovators program by visiting the My Community section of your Developer Profile and clicking “View Settings” for Google Cloud Innovators.

What happens if I opt out of the program emails?

keyboard_arrow_up

You can opt out of the program emails and will remain a member of Innovators unless you explicitly leave the program.

How do I leave the Innovators program?

keyboard_arrow_up

You can find a “Leave group” button by visiting the My Community section of your Developer Profile and clicking “View Settings” for Google Cloud Innovators.

What happens if I update or change the information in the fields required to join the Innovators program?

keyboard_arrow_up

You can choose to update or remove any of those fields from your Developer Profile after you’ve joined the program without affecting your membership status.

How do I sign up to receive program updates on Discord?

keyboard_arrow_up

We are delighted to invite you to the #get-certified-announcements channel on the Google Developers Community Discord server.

How to join:

  1. Join our Discord server: https://discord.gg/google-dev-community. If you're already a member, skip to step 2.
  2. On the left hand side, look for the #get-certified-announcements channel under the "Technologies" section
  3. If you can't see the channel on the left hand side, you can add it to your default view through selecting it in the "Channels & Roles" section. Once there, click "Browse Channels" and search "Get Certified" to follow.

Stay on the lookout for announcements from our community admins!
