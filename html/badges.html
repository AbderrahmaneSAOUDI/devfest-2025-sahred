AI-generated Key Takeaways

  • Google Developer Badges are digital awards that recognize achievements, incentivize participation, and track task completion.

  • Earn badges by completing learning activities, engaging in Google Developer communities and events, and achieving significant milestones.

  • Display your earned badges on your Google Developer Program profile and manage them by adding to favorites, hiding, or deleting them.

  • Publicly share your badges on social media platforms directly from your Google Developer Program profile.

Badges are non-monetary digital awards that recognize developer achievements, incentivize users, and track the completion of specific tasks.

You can earn badges in a variety of ways including creating an account, completing a learning activity such as a codelab or quiz, or participating in Google Developer communities or events.

As a member of the Google Developer Program, you can display your earned badges on your profile and share them on social media if your profile is public.

Earn badges

The following are examples of how you can earn a badge:

  • Completing learning content

    Badges are used to recognize and reward users for passing a quiz or completing a codelab or learning pathway such as Adding Google Maps to a Flutter app or Build a Basic Layout on Android.

  • Completing a meaningful action or milestone

    Badges can hallmark developer actions such as joining the Google Developer Program or completing ten Codelabs.

  • Participating in an event

    Badges can be used to recognize attendance or a notable contribution at a Google sponsored event such as attending Google I/O 2022 or speaking at a student developer conference.

  • Recognizing membership

    Badges can celebrate becoming a member of a community program such as Women Techmakers or becoming a Dev Library Contributor.

Note: If you earn a badge and need to redeem it by voucher code, enter the badge code into the badge redeemer.

Display badges

Your earned badges are displayed under Badges on your Google Developer Program profile.

You can manage your badges by clicking the three-dot menu at the top right corner of any badge and selecting one of the following:

  • Add to favorites

    You can select up to five of your favorite badges to get highlighted at the top of your profile.

  • Hide badge

    Hidden badges are not displayed on your profile.

  • Delete badge

    The badge is removed from your profile.

Share badges

You can share your earned badges on social media if you have a public profile.

To share a badge, do the following:

  1. Open your Google Developer Program profile.

  2. Click the badge you want to share.

    The badge opens in a modal dialog.

  3. In the bottom right corner of the badge, click the icon of the social media platform where you want to share your badge.

    The selected social media platform opens in a new window.
