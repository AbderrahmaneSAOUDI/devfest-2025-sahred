GDG Event Platform

There are over 2000+ Google Developer Groups (GDG) across the globe with over a decade long history of community and events for developers. The GDG event platform is the home for our GDG and GDG on Campus communities.

The GDG event platform is a one-stop-shop for community management; GDG branded website, offering everything at your fingertips; topics, agenda, speakers, sponsors, post-event and feedback forms, scheduled emails and much more to manage your community and your events in the best way possible.

New features

  • June 2025

    Discussions

    Bevy Discussions are AI-powered forums that boost group and event engagement. An AI Copilot helps owners manage content and the community.

    Learn more
  • June 2025

    New Dashboard

    The new GDG Event Platform dashboard is a modern, mobile-friendly experience with AI assistance. The older dashboard remains available during development.

    Learn more
  • October 2023

    New BVC

    Bevy's Virtual Conference (BVC) platform was rebuilt for better reliability, featuring full HD video and AI transcripts. The new interface simplifies event management and attendance.

    Learn more
  • April 2025

    Enhanced Waitlist

    The event waitlist is faster: attendees provide info upfront, allowing organizers to instantly release tickets. Attendees can also easily cancel their RSVP.

    Learn more
  • April 2025

    New Overview Tab

    The new Overview tab is the central event hub, consolidating key details, management, statistics, and a quick link to the post-event wrap-up tab.

    Learn more
  • October 2024

    New RSVP

    The new RSVP Flow streamlines event registration for all users, improving focus, speed, and accuracy. It offers 12 variations, adapting to event needs and preventing overselling.

    Learn more
