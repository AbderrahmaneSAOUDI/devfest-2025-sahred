Welcome to the Community Hub!

The Community Hub serves as the official content repository for Google Developer Experts (GDE), Google Developer Group organizers (GDG), and Google Developer Group on Campus organizers (GDG on Campus), providing program information, resources, and up-to-date content for events.

News and updates

Global GDG program updates

Updated on October 2025
  • Updated Chapter Activity Timelines: The time before a chapter is removed due to inactivity has been shortened from 365 days to 273 days.
  • DevFest Season: New captions for the DevFest Opener video are now available for your use.
  • GDP Premium Trial for GDG Members: To celebrate DevFest, new and existing members in eligible countries can get a 1-month free trial of the Google Developer Program premium.
  • Cloud Skills Boost rebrand: Google Cloud Skills Boost is now Google Skills.
  • Share Anonymous feedback: Don't forget to share your feedback.
  • New pages on the Community Hub: Explore the new Travel and GDG Event Platform Guide pages, plus essential Communication updates on the Community Hub.

GDG organizer cheatsheet

Have you explored all the resources at your fingertips? Find the full list of organizer resources below.

Resource Details
Support & Communication

Local POC ie Regional Lead 

 Your chapter team on the GDG event platform includes your local Regional Lead who you can lean on as your local POC.
On-Demand Support
Email us anytime at gdg-support@google.com; we collaborate closely with your local POCs.
Monthly Newsletter for Organizers

Stay informed on monthly updates by checking out our monthly newsletter or reviewing the archive of past newsletters.
Cross Community Collaboration via Discord

 Connect with the broader Google Developer Community on Discord - join here.
Anonymous Feedback

Share your feedback at goo.gle/gdg25-csat.
Platforms & Tools
Community Leads Platform (CLP)

Check out resources offered to you as a community leads summarized here in your toolkit goo.gle/org-toolkit.
GDG Event Platform
Manage your group on the GDG Event Platform or reach out with questions or requests here: http://goo.gle/gdg-eventplatform-support.
General Event Support
Request support for your events at goo.gle/global-communities-support-request.
Meetup Pro
You can cross-promote your events on Meetup.com  by integrating your Meetup page with your GDG event platform page. Just talk to your regional lead to set this up. Contact us at gdg-support@google.com

Event & Content Resources
Google Skills Access Provide access to Google Skills for your event attendees by requesting access to a share group here - goo.gle/global-communities-support-request.
Google Cloud Console Access (Cloud Credits)
Offer your attendees a credit card less option for accessing the Google Cloud Console by requesting Cloud Credits here - goo.gle/global-communities-support-request.
Technical Content & Organizer Guides
Host great events leveraging content from Google’s product teams curated in our content and event series section.
Affiliate Letters
Solicit sponsorship more effectively with an Affiliate letter by requesting here: goo.gle/global-communities-support-request.
Google Developer Expert Speaker Matching (Googlers coming July)
Google Developer Experts interested in speaking at GDGs are invited to submit sessions. Get connected to a speaker here : goo.gle/google-communities-speaker-matching.
Brand Guides
Review a library of messages, graphics and templates to use when promoting your Google Developer Group: goo.gle/gdg-new-brand-guide.
Grow as a Developer
Google Developer Programs (GDP)
Grow as a developer by creating your profile on the Google Developer Programs (GDP).
Join other programs - Become a GDE
If you're interested in exploring other community programs, such as becoming a Google Developer Expert (GDE), you can find more information here and apply by clicking on your profile in the top right corner of the Advocu platform and then selecting "Become a community member" from the menu.
