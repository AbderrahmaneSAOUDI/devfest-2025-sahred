Google Developer Program

Google Developer Program (GDP) is a platform where developers can learn, build, and grow with Google's products and services. It offers extensive individual learning resources that complement your community work. As a member of Google for Developers programs (GDG, GDE), you receive free access to GDP Premium. This premium access provides exclusive content, advanced learning paths, expert consultations, and specialized tools to help you accelerate your development skills.
Learn more about GDP

How to redeem your free GDP Premium subscription

GDP is a platform for developers to learn, build, and grow with Google's products and services. GDP complements your community work with extensive individual learning resources. Do you already have a profile on the Google Developer Program? If you’re an organizer with accepted program terms and conditions and have a GDP profile, we’ll be assigning you the GDG organizer badge! After you receive the badge you can simply follow these steps to redeem GDP Premium for free:

  1. Navigate to the Google Developer Program Plans & Pricing page.
  2. Click on the "upgrade" button on the premium yearly plan.
  3. Add a payment method (No worries, no charge will be applied, as a 100% discount will be applied at checkout)
    1. Please ensure the 100% discount is applied, and the total cost displayed is $0.00 before completing the purchase. If the discount is not applied and you proceed, you may not be eligible for a refund.

  • That's all!

    • What are the steps for getting my badge?

    As an organizer

    To be eligible to get the GDG or GDG on Campus badge, you need to comply with the following requirements:

    • You are an organizer of a GDG or GDG on Campus chapter
    • You have accepted the program terms and conditions
    • You have a GDP profile with the same email address you use to login to the Event platform

    If you have any issues, feel free to reach out to us at gdg-support@google.com.

    A perk for new and existing members

    GDP Premium FAQs

    What if a GDG/GDGoC member does not see the badge on their profile?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    Make sure you join their local chapter here and the GDG or GDGoC member badge should automatically be awarded to them.

    What exactly is included with Google Developer Program Premium?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    GDP Premium provides enhanced tools, benefits, and support designed to accelerate a developer's career. You can view all the details, including member benefits and features here.

    Does this free trial work for the annual subscription?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    No. This exclusive offer is only valid for the GDP Premium Monthly subscription.

    Why isn't this offer available in every country?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    We're working hard to expand the reach of GDP Premium globally! For now, the free trial is limited to countries where the monthly subscription tier has already launched.

    Is there a special code or coupon GDG members need to use?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    No need for a coupon. The discount is automatically linked to the GDG member badge on their GDP profile. When an eligible user checks out for the monthly subscription, the discount is applied automatically.

    An attendee already has a GDP premium subscription. Can they get a refund to take advantage of the free trial?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    This promotion is intended for new subscribers only. The offer cannot be applied retroactively to an existing subscription.

    A GDG member has the badge on one profile but wants to sign up on another. Is that possible?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    No. The one-month free trial is specifically tied to the GDP profile that received the GDG member badge.

    A GDG member accidentally paid for the first month without the discount. Who should they contact?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    For any billing or subscription support questions, please have them email the dedicated support team at gdp-premium-support@google.com for assistance.

    FAQs

    I need additional help and I don't find the answer to your question. Who should I contact?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    As organizer you can always reach out to your Regional Lead or to us at:

    • gdg-support@google.com for GDG organizers.
    • gdg-on-campus-support@google.com for GDG on Campus organizers.

    As expert you can always reach out to us at:

    • gde-contact@google.com.

    Who is eligible for the GDP Premium discount?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    All GDGoC organizers who have had the GDGoC organizer badge assigned from GDP are eligible for the 100% discount for one year. To gain access, follow the next steps:

    • Ensure you've accepted the Google Terms and Conditions. You'll then be assigned your GDG badge.
    • Visit the Google Developer Program page and create a GDP profile by signing in with the Google account you use as a GDG Organizer.
    • Proceed to upgrade to Premium, and your 100% discount will be visible and applied at checkout.

    Are there any country level restrictions for eligibility?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    To check if GDP Premium is offered in your region, please visit https://developers.google.com/profile/available-regions.

    Does a user need to provide credit card information?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    Yes, a credit card is required to redeem the 100% GDP premium discount for one year.

    I don't have a credit card. What should I do?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    We understand your concern. At this time we require a credit card or debit card in order to sign up for the Google Developer Program annual premium offer.

    When will I get access in my region?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    We appreciate your interest in GDP Premium! To check if it's available in your region, please visit https://developers.google.com/profile/available-regions. We are actively working to expand GDP Premium globally, but specific launch dates for new countries are not yet available due to various factors involved in country-specific rollouts.

    Will it be without a credit card at all?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    No, it is generally not possible to access GDP Premium without providing credit or debit card information.

    While you will not be charged, and no actual payment is processed during initial setup, a valid credit or debit card is required for account verification and to enable seamless access to the service.

    Will I get a reminder once my 1 year marks to the end?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    Yes, Google Developer Program sends a renewal notification when your active subscription is about to renew.

    Can I get a refund if I forgot to cancel my membership?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    Program fees are non refundable. You can read more in our GDP FAQ.

    What is this discount applicable to?

    keyboard_arrow_up

    This discount is exclusively available for the Yearly Premium plan.
