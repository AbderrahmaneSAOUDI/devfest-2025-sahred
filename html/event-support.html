Event support

GDG organizers can request support for their events by using the Global Google Communities Support Request form below. Feel free to explore our support category options to see what we can offer. Learn more below.

Please note: Completing this form does not guarantee that your support request will be approved.

Questions: gdg-support@google.com

Support categories

Request support for current event series as well as stand-alone meetups via the form here. Your regional lead/community manager will follow up with you regarding your request.

You can request support for the following category/categories:

  • Catering
  • Internet/Online Event Support
  • Event Displays/Signage (i.e. printing a banner, printing signs, etc.)
  • Event Space Rental
  • Swag (i.e. attendee giveaways, speaker appreciation gifts)
  • Travel Grants
  • Affiliate Letter
  • Cloud resources

Note: You can request Cloud resources only for published events.

Requesting Google Skills and Cloud Console access

All requests must be submitted via the event support form at least 15 days before the event.

Google Skills access

  • What it is: A learning platform with pre-selected paths.
  • Duration: One year of access for you and your attendees.
  • How to request:
    • Use the event support form and select "Google Skills access."
    • You must have a Google Skills account created beforehand.
  • How it works: You will receive a confirmation email from noreply@cloudskillsboost.google with instructions on how to complete your registration. Please follow the steps outlined in the email to complete your registration.

Google Cloud Console access

  • What it is: Credit-based access to the Google Cloud Console without a credit card.
  • Duration: Valid for the length of the event only.
  • How to request:
    • Use the event support form and select "Cloud Console access."
    • Request a maximum of 200 credits.
    • For virtual attendees, RSVPs are required on the GDG Event Platform at least five days prior.
  • How it works: You will receive a redemption link 2-5 days before the event to share with attendees. The link becomes inactive three days after the event.

For all requests: You will need your organizer email and the published event link from the GDG Event Platform. For questions or support, email gdg-support@google.com.

FAQs

If you do not find the answer to your question, reach out to your Regional Lead or to gdg-support@google.com.

Can I use my Cloud Console access code for a rescheduled event?

keyboard_arrow_up

No, your existing Cloud Console access code is tied to your original event date. If you need to reschedule, you must contact gdg-support@google.com to request an adjustment. Please make sure you've already updated the new event dates on the GDG Event platform before you reach out to the support team. This will ensure a smoother and faster process for getting your updated code.

Can I request Cloud Console for my online event?

keyboard_arrow_up

Yes, you can. For virtual events, we pre-register attendees based on the RSVP list on the GDG Event Platform we have when your unique link is generated. If someone registers after the link is sent to you, their email will not be on the pre-registered list.

I collected RSVPs for my virtual event on an external platform, not the GDG Event Platform. Can I still get access to Cloud Console for my virtual event?

keyboard_arrow_up

Yes! To ensure your attendees don't have to register again, we recommend you bulk upload them on the GDG Event Platform. You can do this by creating a simple CSV document with just three essential columns:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
Once you have this CSV file ready, simply submit a ticket via goo.gle/gdg-eventplatform-support, and the support team will take care of adding them for you.

After that, we'll be able to pre-register your attendees, and you'll be all set! Please remember to do that at least 5 days before the event; otherwise, please reach out to gdg-support@google.com.

How attendees can access Google Cloud Console?

keyboard_arrow_up

Attendees can access the Google Cloud Console using only a Gmail account. Review and share this deck for additional details on how your attendees can sign up with their Google Account and get access to the Free Tier.

Does the organizer need to have an account on the Google Skills platform?

keyboard_arrow_up

Organizers: You'll need to create an account on the Google Skills platform first. This ensures they receive the invitation email).
Attendees: Anyone you add to the group will also need an account to participate.

How to add attendees to the Google Skills group?

keyboard_arrow_up

To add individual members to your group on the Google Skills platform:

  1. Log in to the platform.
  2. Navigate to your group.
  3. Click on "Members" in the left-side sidebar.
  4. Select "Add individual members."
  5. For bigger groups, you can also upload attendees in a CSV file.

I've added my attendees to my Google Skills group, but they aren't appearing. What should I do?

keyboard_arrow_up

Your attendees may not have received your invitations because their Google Skills accounts were not created prior to the invitation. Please ask them to create their accounts at https://www.cloudskillsboost.google, and then try sending the invitations again.

What are Google Skills sharing groups?

keyboard_arrow_up

Sharing groups are a way to organize users and provide access to specific learning paths. These groups are managed directly by their organizers, who have the authority to add and remove members. When an organizer requests Google Skills, they gain access to their events on the Google Skills platform and can add attendees. This allows organizers to manage their events more effectively and ensure that only authorized individuals can participate.

What learning pathways are available?

keyboard_arrow_up

Available learning paths:

  • Google Cloud Computing Foundations Certificate
  • Beginner: Introduction to Generative AI Learning Path
  • Advanced: Generative AI for Developers Learning Path
  • Intermediate: Gemini for Google Cloud Learning Path
  • Beginner: Google Cloud Cybersecurity Certificate
  • Labs for Build w/AI
  • Integrate Generative AI Into Your Data Workflow
  • Build and Modernize Applications With Generative AI
  • Deploy and Manage Generative AI Models
  • Generate Smarter Generative AI Outputs

Can I add co-organizers to the Google Skills sharing group?

keyboard_arrow_up

Organizers cannot add co-organizers themselves. If a co-organizer is needed, submit a request to gdg-support@google.com.

Where can I find the brand guide & assets?

keyboard_arrow_up

GDG branding resources can be found here.

Can I co-host my event with another chapter NOT in my region?

keyboard_arrow_up

Absolutely! Collaborate with any active chapter in the program to co-host your event. Just remember to factor in time zone differences during the planning stage.
